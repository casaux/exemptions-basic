Exemptions: COVID-19


To facilitate continued operation of the aviation industry during the COVID-19 pandemic, CASA has exempted for three months:

  • provisions of Part 61 flight crew licencing
  • training and checking provisions of CAR 217
  • training and checking provisions of CAR 215.

    • The exemption also extends the expiry dates for three months for:

  • extended the proficiency check for emergency procedures
  • dangerous goods training
  • DAMP training.

    • The exemption, CASA EX57/20 also allows operators to develop alternate training and checking procedures to ensure their operating crew remain competent from 1 July 2020.

    CASA has not exempted recent experience requirements, other than those under regulation 61.1055, and therefore flight crew will need to meet recent experience requirements before conducting AOC operations.
